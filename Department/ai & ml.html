ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING ENGINEERING

The department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Engineering was established in the year 2020 with an objective of training students and to keep pace with the present IT trends . The department is currently headed by Dr. N P Kavya with an experience of 26 years in the field of education and research. The department offers AI & ML course which is a Graduate Degree. Current intake for AI & ML Programme is 60. The duration of the course from 2020 admission, is for 4 years(8 Semesters). The AI & ML programme at RNSIT is approved by AICTE affiliated to VTU(Visvesvaraya Technological University). The department in collaboration with CSE Department has a VTU recognized R & D centre. The department has Well structured laboratories, library facility for staff & students.

Department of AI & ML is structured for Excelling in teaching learning methodology. The department will train students so that they have consistent good academic track record also gives priority to practical perspective. The Wi-Fi enabled campus helps the student to pursue academic activities in a vast and peaceful environment.

Periodical Technical guest lectures will be arranged to make the students to be in par with current IT trends. The department organises Faculty development programs to TRAIN the TRAINERS on regular basis. Student development programs will also organised to train students on current industry requirements like AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, IOT, Python Programming, Cloud Computing, Android Development, Web Technologies etc.

Annual newsletter will be designed & Published to reflect activities & achivements of the department.
